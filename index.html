"UP"

(Made By Disney - Pixar, Released in the U.S: May 29, 2009)

The film is focused on Carl Fredricksen, an elderly man who dreams of travelling to South America in order to fulfill a promise that he made to his deceased wife Ellie. Accidentally, Russell, a scout wishing to earn a specific badge travels with him on accident. They encounter several other characters along the way, such as a talking dog named "Dug", and a large, supposedly extinct bird named "Kevin". During this journey, Carl learns and understands more about the world that he saw as a kid, and understands the true meaning of promises, families, and adventures.
